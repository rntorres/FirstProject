All About Koalas

What Koalas Eat

Koalas primarily eat eucalyptus leaves.

Koalas have a unique digestive system that can break down these leaves.

Baby Koalas

What Are Baby Koalas Called?

Baby koalas are called joeys.

How Do Baby Koalas Look When They're Born?

Joeys are born without fur.

Quick Facts About Koalas

  • Koalas are marsupials.
  • Koalas are found in the forests of eastern Australia.
  • Koalas can sleep up to 18 hours a day!
