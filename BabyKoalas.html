Life of a Koala Joey

As we previously learned, baby koalas are called joeys.
Now let's look at a table that charts the growth of a Joey in the first stages of life.

Months Phase of Life
1-6 The joey is living in the pouch of its mom.
7-10 The joey begins peeking outside of the pouch.
12+ The joey still feeds from mother, but begins to become more independent.
